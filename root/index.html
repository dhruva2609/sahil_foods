a world about us

Sahil Foods has been the bakery and pastry shop in Rome since 1986, offering only the best of baked goods. All our creations are prepared with genuine ingredients and without preservatives, carefully selected to always offer the highest quality.

Cakes, tarts, seasonal and bar products and above all our specialty the donut are fresh, produced daily and in a natural way, to give you a delicious and above all healthy taste experience. For over fifty years we have been suppliers of confectionery and bakery products to bars and restaurants.
